It generates terms by number of times you click the button. The "clear" button clear everything generated and reset to initial condition. Fib

The Fibonacci sequence is sequence in which each number is the sum of preceding ones.

The sequence starts with first two numbers T(1)=0, T(2)=1. The more general way of denoting the logic is,

T(n)=T(n-1)+T(n-2).

T(3)=T(2)+T(1)

The T(n) represents the nth term (i.e. like first, second, third...).
